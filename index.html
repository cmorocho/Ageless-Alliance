Fighting Elder Abuse

Ageless Link, partnered with Ageless Alliance, is a non-profit website solution to bridge volunteers with isolated elders as a prevention for elder abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

Reported Abuse Cases

Neglect accounts for over 50% of elder abuse reports.

Why Should You Join?

  • Elder Abuse is Harmful

    Elder abuse can lead to serious physical injuries and long-term psychological consequences. Those who have poor social connections and do not participate in social activities are at an increased risk of cognitive decline.

  • Social Isolation increases risk

    Studies have shown that socially isolated individuals are more likely to experience maltreatment in the form of physical and emotional abuse.

  • Elder Abuse Cases May Double

    The global population of people aged 60 years and older will more than double, from 900 million in 2015 to about 2 billion in 2050.

  • Social Networking is a positive influence

    An older adult's social network can impact health positively through encouragement to adhere to medical treatment or to refrain from negative or risky behaviors.
